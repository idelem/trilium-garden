simplistic english

simplistic english is an opinionated english idiolect coined by ME, used by ME, mostly in my personal notes.

characteristics:

  • doesn't like capitalization
  • doesn't like subjects
  • has a tiny vocabulary, often coupled with wrong/inauthentic/creative/call-it-what-you-like usage

why:

  • it is encoded in ascii, thus compatible with most note-taking tools
  • as the name suggests, it has simplistic syntax
  • compiles very fast
  • little to no memory overhead
  • and still human-readable
  • yet introduces a layer of security, protecting the message from annoying, over-your-shoulder readers
  • (various other advantages, TBC)
